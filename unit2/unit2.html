The best soda out there is for sure a big debate, but there are a few choices that everyone can agree are some of the top dogs of soda.

Some of these top sodas are as follow:

  • Dr. Pepper
  • Coke
  • Pepsi
  • Mountain Dew

Now which sodas are the best in my professional opinion? Well, here is how I would rank some of these sodas comapred to each other:

  1. Dr. Pepper > Mountain Dew
  2. Dr. Pepper <' Coke
  3. Coke = Pepsi
  4. Coke & Pepsi > Dr. Pepper & Mountain Dew
