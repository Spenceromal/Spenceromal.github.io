Chicago Cubs

Overview

The Chicago Cubs are one of the most well-known teams in all of baseball. The team has been around since 1870 and they still play at one of the oldest ballparks still around, Wrigleyfield, which was created in 1914.

Cubs website

Championships

The Chicago Cubs have won 3 World Series chamopionships over their long history. The most recent of them being in 2016.
Before winning in 2016, they had not won the World Series since 1908. Here are all of their championship winning seasons:

  • 1907
  • 1908
  • 2016

2016 Postseason run

During the Cubs historic postseason run in 2016, the Cubs had to face 3 teams to become world champions, here were those teams in the order they played them:

  1. San Franciso Giants (Beat in 4 games in best of 5 series)
  2. Los Angeles Dodgers (Beat in 6 games in best of 7 series)
  3. Cleveland Indians (Beat in 7 games in best of 7 series)
